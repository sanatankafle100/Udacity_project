Black Mamba

Black Mamba is the fastest snake in the world.

  • Scientific Name: Dendroaspis polylepis
  • Average Length: 8.2 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years
  • Habitat: savannas and rocky hills of southern and eastern Africa

Black Mamba has a black mouth which is why it is named as BLACK mamba. It is the second longest snake and one of the most aggressive one. Most people including experts fear against this snake due to it's speed, toxicity and most importantly it's aggression.
